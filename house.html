Aliana Events is a luxury, haute couture inspired event planning company founded by Aliana Rubins.

Aliana started her career in the US as a makeup artist in the fashion and beauty industry, helping launch Giorgio Armani Beauty.

After ten years in the beauty industry, Aliana started her event planning company in 2013. Inspired by runway shows, fashion, and glamour, Aliana creates a fantasy world filled with luxury for her clients.

Her meticulous attention to detail and creative direction has drawn the attention of high profile magazines like Vogue, Grace Ormande.

In February she was named 204 Events’ featured designer of the month.

Aliana’s world is all about aesthetic; taking comprehensive ideas and creating beautiful parties, exquisite dinners, and breathtaking realities.

Aliana has created a following of prestigious clients including The Housewives of Europe.
